You don't necessarily have to know details about every crop.The purpose of the crop advisory is to ensure right information and knowledge on crop production that is actionable is delivered to the farming household so that they are able to take action in time to achieve steady crop productivity and production.

Types of Seasons

Kharif

The cropping season in India starts in June and ends in October where monsoon crops are cultivated and harvested

Rabi

The season where crops are sown in mid-November and harvested in April/May is called Rabi Season.

Zaid

Since the Zaid crops are also called summer crops, they are sown and harvested between March and June.

How are we doing this?

Our chatbot uses a weather API to fetch the 7-day forecast and will translate the information to the farmers. The farmer will be able to communicate with the chatbot with whatever language he is comfortable with since we will have language translation. Our chatbot will even include information on how to yield better output, modern farming methods and helpline for various emergencies.
